# Bangalore Traffic Knowledge Base

Bangalore is known for heavy traffic congestion, especially during peak hours.

Peak Hours:
- Morning: 8:00 AM – 11:00 AM
- Evening: 5:30 PM – 9:00 PM

Traffic Characteristics:
- IT corridors like Whitefield, Electronic City, ORR, and Bellandur are highly congested.
- Metro construction and narrow roads increase delays.
- Rain significantly worsens traffic conditions.

Average Traffic Estimates:
- Whitefield to Electronic City:
  - Peak hours: 2 to 2.5 hours
  - Non-peak: 1.25 hours
- Marathahalli to Silk Board:
  - Peak hours: 1.5 to 2 hours
  - Non-peak: 40 minutes
- Yelahanka to Majestic:
  - Peak hours: 1.5 hours
  - Non-peak: 45 minutes

Local Knowledge:
- Silk Board junction is one of the most congested spots.
- Two-wheelers can sometimes move faster than cars.
- Traffic police diversions are common during peak hours.

Language & Culture:
- Locals often say “traffic is crazy”, “signal signal”, or “only traffic boss”.
